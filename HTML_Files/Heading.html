A Paragaph consists of one more sentences that form a self-contained unit of discourse. The start of a paragaph is indicated by a new line.

Text is eaiser to understand when it is split up into unit of text. For example, a book may of chapters. Chapters can have subheadings. Under each heading there will be one or more paragaphs.
