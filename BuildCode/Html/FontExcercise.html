Briards

by Ivay Duckett

The briard, or berger de brie, is a large breed of dog traditionally used as a herder and guardian of sheep

Breed History

The briard which is belived to have orginated in France, has been bred for centuries to herf and to protect sheep. The breed was used by the French Army as sentries, messengers and to search for wounded soldiers because of its fine sense of hearing. Briads were used in the First World War almost to the point of extinction.
